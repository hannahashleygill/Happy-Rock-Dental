MEET OUR TEAM

Let us introduce you to the friendly staff at Happy Rock Dental.

TAMMY
Front Office
Tammy has been working as a dental receptionist since 1998, and she has been working in our office since 2005. Her tasks include processing insurance claims, scheduling, and other office duties. Because of her experience she has knowledge of the many insurance situations which may arise, and is very proficient in answering all of your questions concerning coverage. She lives in Gresham with her husband and children. Feel free to call Tammy with any questions you may have.
KARLIE
Front Office
Karlie works part time in the front office and has been with us since 2009. Karlie's duties include answering phone calls and scheduling. she is also involved with monitoring and posting for the practice’s social media which includes Facebook and Twitter. Karlie is also Dr. Bob’s daughter, and it is very nice to have her working with us.
PAMELA
Hygienist
Pam graduated from Mt Hood Community College with an Associate’s degree in Applied Science in 2004. Pam has worked in the dental field since 1987, and as a dental hygienist since 2005. She is experienced in the many aspects of the dental office which include general, orthodontics, and pediatric dentistry. The first eighteen years of her career were spent as a dental assistant for a children’s dental practice. Pam is certified in CPR/BLS training. She continues to further her knowledge every year by maintaining at least twelve hours of continuing education courses.
CINDY
Hygienist
Cindy has worked at the same family friendly dental office in Gladstone, Oregon since 1971, with Dr. Everett’s team since 1991. She graduated from the University of Oregon Dental School in 1970 and was presented the outstanding Clinical Achievement Award in dental hygiene. She specializes in periodontal treatment and quality periodontal maintenance for her patients. Cindy has enjoyed working with Dr Everett’s dental team for the last 22 years. Cindy has two children and lives with her husband in Oregon City.
MELISSA
Dental Asisstant
Melissa is a Certified Dental Assistant who has been with the practice since 2002. She received her education from Portland Community College. Melissa is the lead assistant and handles not only assisting duties with care, but also all of the inventory maintenance and ordering. In addition, she is the practice’s OSHA and orthodontic treatment coordinator. Melissa is a very caring individual, who always strives to create a pleasant and comfortable atmosphere.
CARRIE
Dental Asisstant
An Oregonian all her life, Carrie enjoyes all the superb outdoor activities that it has to offer. Camping and swimming at the river are her favorite. Carrie has been a Dental Assistant in Dr. Everett’s office for 10 years. She enjoys communicating with patients and informing them of their dental needs. As a resident of Gladstone she believes it is a real pleasure not only to work in this community, but to meet the people who live here. Feel free to come by and say hello to her.
